Being a great engineer takes much more than writing code. Soft Skills Engineering is a weekly podcast for software developers who want to level up their engineering skills outside the realm of writing code. Our hosts, Jamison Dance and Dave Smith, are here to make you laugh and make you wise as they answer your questions, dear listeners, on the air each week.

Hosts

Dave Smith

Dave has 15 years of experience as a software developer. He served as director of engineering at HireVue where he was responsible for a team of 40 software developers and QA engineers. Dave currently works on Amazon Alexa in Boston.

Jamison Dance

Jamison is a code whisperer and experienced product engineer. He has led teams and been led. He thinks you are great.
